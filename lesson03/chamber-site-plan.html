São José dos Campos Chamber Planning Site

Site Purpose

The São José dos Campos Chamber Planning Site aims to outline the goals and specifications for the development and running of the chamber's website. It serves as a guide for the design and content creation process.

Scenarios

  • What kind of entertainment does this city promote?
  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
